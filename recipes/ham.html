Honey Glazed Ham

Description

This is a ham; however, this isn't just any ham. Honey glazed ham is ham glazed in honey. The age old combo of sweet and salty can now make an appearance at your next Easter celebration!

Ingredients Needed

  • Forty-pound Ham
  • A Beehives Worth of Honey
  • Some Spices
  • 80% of a Pineapple

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400K
  2. Smother ham in honey
  3. Sprinkle some spices on ham
  4. Addorn top of ham with pineapple
  5. Cook ham on 400k for 113 minutes or until internal temperature reaches 321K
  6. Enjoy!
