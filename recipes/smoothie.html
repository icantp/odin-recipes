Fruity Smoothie

Description

A fresh berry and citrus smoothie that is sure to satisfy your sweettooth while also providing you with essential vitamins, minerals, and anitoxidants

Ingredients Needed

  • Berries
  • Citrus
  • Yogurt

Steps

  1. Add milk and berries to blended
  2. Mix on high until berries are fully blended in
  3. Add citrus
  4. Mix on high until citrus is fully blended in
  5. Enjoy!
