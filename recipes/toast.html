Buttered Toast al Dente

Description

The internet in 2022 is filled to the brim with subreddits, recipe websites, and bloggers all claiming to have solved buttered toast. All of these different resources create a lot of indecisiveness when it comes to selecting the ideal buttered toast recipe. Well, you can stop looking and start eating. This is the one and only toast recipe you will ever need.

Ingredients

  • Toast
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Put toast in toaster
  2. Take toast out when it's toasted to your liking
  3. Using a butter knife, spread butter evenly across the surface of warm toast
  4. Take a picture to add to your Instagram Story
  5. Create an Instagram Story with the picture of your toast, a trendy filter, and a caption that reads "adulting isn't that hard lol"
  6. After posting, your butter should be sufficiently melted
  7. Enjoy!
